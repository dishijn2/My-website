Paper Quilled Art Roundup

The art of paper quilling has always mesmerized me. There are a lot of artists today making gorgeous and interesting work with this humble art form. For those who are unfamiliar with this technique, it involves the use of strips of paper that are rolled, shaped, and glued together to create decorative designs. They can range from wall hangings, to cards, to 3D figurines and even jewelry. Take a look at this roundup of paper quilled projects, though if you throw this term into a search engine, you will find so many more beautiful designs. You can also check out this simple how-to guide to get started on your own projects.
